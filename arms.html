Arms Exercises

Your guide to effective arm workouts

Bicep Curls

  1. Stand up straight with a dumbbell in each hand at arm's length.
  2. Raise one dumbbell and twist your forearm until it is vertical and your palm faces the shoulder.
  3. Lower to original position and repeat with opposite arm

Tricep Pushdowns

Tricep pushdowns are excellent for working the triceps. Remember, tricpes make 75% of your arms so make sure to work them effectively!

  1. Stand one step away from the cable pulley, and grip a bar about shoulder-width apart.
  2. Pull the handle down until your upper arms are perpendicular to the floor. This is the starting position.
  3. Push the handle down until your arms are fully extended.
  4. With control, let the handle up again.

Hammer Curls

Hammer curls target the brachialis, a muscle that lies beneath the biceps.

  1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and hold dumbbells with a neutral grip.
  2. Keep your upper arms still and bend your elbows to curl the dumbbells up towards your shoulders.
  3. Twist your little finger side of your hands inward at the top of the movement to engage the brachialis muscle.
  4. Pause for a brief moment at the top of the curl, then slowly lower the dumbbells back to the starting position.
